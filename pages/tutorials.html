Clickable Images

There’s an image on your page, or maybe it’s a blog type and there’s 30 of them. You’d like to allow your readers to click them, have them load on a new page, and become a large size.

You’ve googled for a while and can’t find it.

So here’s an example:

Print below or above the image “Click on an image to see bigger image” type a href=”example.jpg” and img src=”example.jpg” and….that’s it. Just do it for each image you have.
