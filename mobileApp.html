I was asked to show the steps I would take to build a mobile application for a large company.

This project was very generic and broad, but allowed me to have creative control and think of ways to motivate a team as well as build an application.

The major difficulty with this project was not hving much direction as to what the company was looking for in a mobile app. They did not specify what they wanted to application to do or if they wanted it to work cross platform or only on Android or iPhones and iPads.

In designing this presentation, the company requested I use PowerPoint. If I had my choice, I would have used Prezi.

In working to create this presentation, I used my project management skills to think of how I would manage both the project and people.
